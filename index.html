Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before we get started.

What kind of businesses do you work with?
We work with all kinds of local businesses — restaurants, cafés, salons, gyms, boutiques, dental clinics, plumbers, photographers, and more. If you serve customers in your area and need a professional online presence, we can help.
How long does it take to build my website?
Landing pages are typically ready in 3–5 days. Full business websites launch in about 7 days. E-commerce stores take 10–14 days depending on the number of products. The timeline starts once you've provided your content (text, photos, logo).
Do I need to provide photos and text?
Ideally yes — your own photos and words always make a site feel more authentic. But if you don't have them, we can source high-quality stock photos and write the copy for you — just let us know and we'll sort it out together.
Will my site show up on Google?
Every site we build includes on-page SEO setup — proper headings, meta tags, fast load speed, and Google Business integration. This gives you a solid foundation to rank locally. SEO takes time, but you'll be starting in the right position from day one.
What happens after the site goes live?
You own the site completely. We'll walk you through how to make basic updates yourself. If you need ongoing changes or maintenance, we offer flexible support packages — just get in touch.
